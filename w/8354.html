šātā שָׁתָה ‘to drink’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: שׁתה | Morphology: H:V

1) to drink
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to drink
1a1a) of drinking cup of God's wrath, of slaughter, of wicked deeds (fig)
1a2) to feast
1b) (Niphal) to be drunk
