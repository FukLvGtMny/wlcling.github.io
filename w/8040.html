śǝmōʾl שְׂמֹאל ‘left’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive word (rather perhaps from the same as H8071 (by insertion of the aleph) through the idea of wrapping up) | Roots: שׂמלה | Morphology: N-M

śǝmōʾl
1) the left, the left hand, the left side
1a) left
1b) left hand
1c) north (as one faces east)
