rāšāʿ רָשָׁע ‘wicked’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7561 | Roots: רשׁע | Morphology: A

rāšāʿ
1) wicked, criminal
1a) guilty one, one guilty of crime (subst)
1b) wicked (hostile to God)
1c) wicked, guilty of sin (against God or man)
