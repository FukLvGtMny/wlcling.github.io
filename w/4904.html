miškāb מִשְׁכָּב ‘bed’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7901

Roots: שׁכב

In another language: שׁכב

Morphology: H:N-M

1) a lying down, couch, bier, act of lying
1a) couch, bed
1b) act of lying, lying down or sleeping room, bedroom
1c) lying down (for sexual contact)
