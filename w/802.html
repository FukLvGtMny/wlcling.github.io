ʾiššā אִשָּׁה ‘woman’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: feminine of H376 or H582 | Roots: אישׁ אנושׁ אנושׁ | In another language: אישׁ | Morphology: H:N-F

1) woman, wife, female
1a) woman (opposite of man)
1b) wife (woman married to a man)
1c) female (of animals)
1d) each, every (pronoun)
