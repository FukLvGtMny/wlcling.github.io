bǝhēmâ בְּהֵמָה ‘animal’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root (probably meaning to be mute) | In another language: בהמה | Morphology: H:N-F

1) beast, cattle, animal
1a) beasts (coll of all animals)
1b) cattle, livestock (of domestic animals)
1c) wild beasts
