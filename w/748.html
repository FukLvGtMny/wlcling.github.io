ʾārax אָרַךְ ‘to prolong’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Morphology: V

ʾārax
1) to be long, prolong
1a) (Qal) to be long
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to prolong (days)
1b2) to make long (tent cords)
1b3) to grow long, continue long
