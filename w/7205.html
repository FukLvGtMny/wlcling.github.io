rǝʾûbēn רְאוּבֵן ‘Reuben’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the imperative of H7200 and H1121 | Roots: ראה בן | In another language: ראה | Morphology: N:N-M-P

Reuben = "behold a son"
1) the eldest son of Jacob by Leah
2) the tribe descended from Reuben
3) the territory inhabited by the tribe of Reuben
