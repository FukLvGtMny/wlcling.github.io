hālak הָלַךְ ‘to go’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: akin to H3212

Roots: ילך

In another language: הלך

Morphology: H:V

Meaning
1) to go, walk, come
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to go, walk, come, depart, proceed, move, go away
1a2) to die, live, manner of life (fig.)
1b) (Piel)
1b1) to walk
1b2) to walk (fig.)
1c) (Hithpael)
1c1) to traverse
1c2) to walk about
1d) (Niphal) to lead, bring, lead away, carry, cause to walk
