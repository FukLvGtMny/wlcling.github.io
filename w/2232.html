zāraʿ זָרַע ‘to sow’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: זרע | Morphology: H:V

1) to sow, scatter seed
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to sow
1a2) producing, yielding seed
1b)(Niphal)
1b1) to be sown
1b2) to become pregnant, be made pregnant
1c) (Pual) to be sown
1d) (Hiphil) to produce seed, yield seed
