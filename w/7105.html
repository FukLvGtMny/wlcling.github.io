qāṣîr קָצִיר ‘1harvest 2foliage’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7114 | In another language: קצר | Variants: קָצִיר, קָצִיר | Morphology: H:N-M

A 1) harvest, harvesting
1a) process of harvesting
1b) crop, what is harvested or reaped
1c) time of harvest B 1) boughs, branches
