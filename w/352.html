ʾayil אַ֫יִל ‘1ram 2pillar 3leader 4terebinth’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the same as H193 | Roots: אול | In another language: אול | Variants: אַ֫יִל, אַ֫יִל | Morphology: H:N-M

A 1) ram
1a) ram (as food)
1b) ram (as sacrifice)
1c) ram (skin dyed red, for tabernacle)
B 1) pillar, door post, jambs, pilaster
C 1) strong man, leader, chief
D 1) mighty tree, terebinth
