ʾōt אוֹת ‘sign’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably from H225 (in the sense of appearing) | Roots: אות | In another language: אות | Morphology: H:N-F

1) sign, signal
1a) a distinguishing mark
1b) banner
1c) remembrance
1d) miraculous sign
1e) omen
1f) warning
2) token, ensign, standard, miracle, proof
