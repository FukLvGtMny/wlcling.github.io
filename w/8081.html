šemen שֶׁ֫מֶן ‘oil’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H8080 | Roots: שׁמן | In another language: שׁמן | Morphology: H:N-M

1) fat, oil
1a) fat, fatness
1b) oil, olive oil
1b1) as staple, medicament or unguent
1b2) for anointing
1c) fat (of fruitful land, valleys) (metaph)
