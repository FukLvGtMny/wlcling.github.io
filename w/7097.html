qāṣeh קָצֶה ‘(a) end (b) end’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7096 | Roots: קצה | In another language: קצה | Variants: קֵ֫צֶה, קָצֶה | Morphology: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

(a) 1) end, extremity
1a) end, mouth, extremity
1b) border, outskirts
1c) the whole (condensed term for what is included within extremities)
1d) at the end of (a certain time) (b) 1) end
