pûṣ פּוּץ ‘(a) to scatter (b) scatterer (c) to flow (d) to shatter’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root

In another language: פוץ

Variants: פּוּץ, מֵפִיץ, פָּצַץ

Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:N-M (c) H:V (d) H:V

Meaning
(a) 1) to scatter, be dispersed, be scattered
1a) (Qal) to be dispersed, be scattered
1b) (Niphal)
1b1) to be scattered
1b2) to be spread abroad
1c) (Hiphil) to scatter
1d) Hithpael) scatter (b) n.m.
1) scatterer (c) 1) (Qal) to flow, overflow (d) 1) to break
1a) (Polel) to shatter
1b) (Pilpel) to dash to pieces
