šāxōl שָׁכֹל ‘1be bereaved 2barren 3barenness’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Variants: שָׁכֹל, שָׁכוּל, מְשַׁכֶּ֫לֶת | Morphology: 1V 2A 3N-F

A 1) to be bereaved, make childless, miscarry
1a) (Qal) to be bereaved
1b) (Piel)
1b1) to make childless
1b2) to cause barrenness, show barrenness or abortion
1b3) to miscarry
1c) (Hiphil) miscarrying (participle)
B adj
1) childless (through bereavement)
C 1) barrenness
