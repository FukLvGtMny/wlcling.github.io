ḥēleb חֵ֫לֶב ‘fat’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to be fat | Morphology: H:N-M

1) fat
1a) fat (of humans)
1b) fat (of beasts)
1c) choicest, best part, abundance (of products of the land)
