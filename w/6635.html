ṣābāʾ צָבָא ‘(a) army (b) Hosts’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H6633 | Roots: צבא | In another language: צבא | Variants: צָבָא, צָבָא | Morphology: (a) H:N-M (b) N:N-M-T

(a) 1) that which goes forth, army, war, warfare, host
1a) army, host
1a1) host (of organised army)
1a2) host (of angels)
1a3) of sun, moon, and stars
1a4) of whole creation
1b) war, warfare, service, go out to war
1c) service (b) 1) The Hosts (of the Lord)
