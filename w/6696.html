ṣūr צוּר ‘1to confine 2to provoke 3to form’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: צור | Variants: צוּר, צוּר | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to bind, besiege, confine, cramp
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to confine, secure
1a2) to shut in, beseige
1a3) to shut up, enclose
B 1) (Qal) to show hostility to, be an adversary, treat as foe
C 1) (Qal) to form, fashion, delineate
