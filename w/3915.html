laylâ לַ֫יְלָה ‘night’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the same as H3883

Roots: לול

In another language: לול

Variants: H:N-M

Meaning
1) night
1a) night (as opposed to day)
1b) of gloom, protective shadow (fig.)
