ʾavrāhām אַבְרָהָם ‘Abraham’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: contracted from H1 and an unused root (probably meaning to be populous) | Roots: אב | In another language: אב (1414) | Morphology: N:N-M-P

Abraham = "father of a multitude" or "chief of multitude"
1) friend of God and founder of Hebrew nation via God's elective covenant
