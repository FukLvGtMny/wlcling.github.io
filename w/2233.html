zeraʿ זֶ֫רַע ‘seed’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2232 | Roots: זרע | In another language: זרע | Morphology: H:N-M

1) seed, sowing, offspring
1a) a sowing
1b) seed
1c) semen virile
1d) offspring, descendants, posterity, children
1e) of moral quality
1e1) a practitioner of righteousness (fig.)
1f) sowing time (by meton)
