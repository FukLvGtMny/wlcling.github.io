bilʿām בִּלְעָם ‘(a) Balaam (b) Bileam’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably from H1077 and H5971 | Roots: בל עם | Variants: בִּלְעָם, בִּלְעָם | Morphology: (a) N:N-M-P (b) N:N--L

(a) Balaam = "not of the people"
n pr m
1) the son of Beor, a man endowed with the gift of prophecy (b) Balaam = "not of the people"
n pr loc
1) a town in Manasseh
