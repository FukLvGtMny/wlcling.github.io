ʾĕlōhîm אֱלֹהִים ‘God’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: plural of H433 | Roots: אלוה אלוה | In another language: אול | Morphology: H:N-M

1) (plural)
1a) rulers, judges
1b) divine ones
1c) angels
1d) gods
2) (plural intensive-singular meaning)
2a) god, goddess
2b) godlike one
2c) works or special possessions of God
2d) the (true) God
2e) God
