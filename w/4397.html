malʾāxǝ מַלְאָךְ ‘messenger’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to despatch as a deputy | In another language: מלאך | Morphology: H:N-M

1) messenger, representative
1a) messenger
1b) angel
1c) the theophanic angel
