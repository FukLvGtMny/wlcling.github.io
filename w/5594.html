sāpad סָפַד ‘to mourn’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: ספד | Morphology: H:V

1) to wail, lament, mourn
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to wail, lament
1a2) wailers (participle)
1b) (Niphal) to be lamented, be bewailed
