gepen גֶּ֫פֶן ‘vine’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to bend | Morphology: H:N-M

1) vine, vine tree
1a) of Israel (fig.)
1b) of stars fading at Jehovah's judgment (metaph.)
1c) of prosperity
