Etymology: from H6030 | Roots: ענה | In another language: ענה | Morphology: H:PREP + H:CONJ

1) purpose, intent prep
1a) for the sake of
1b) in view of, on account of
1c) for the purpose of, to the intent that, in order to conj
1d) to the end that
