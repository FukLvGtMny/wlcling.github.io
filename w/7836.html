šāḥar שָׁחַר ‘to seek’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root

In another language: שׁחר

Morphology: H:V

1) to seek, seek early or earnestly, look early or diligently for
1a) (Qal) to look for diligently, seek
1b) (Piel) to seek, seek early
