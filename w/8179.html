šaʿar שַׁ֫עַר ‘gate’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H8176 in its original sense | Roots: שׁער | In another language: שׁער | Morphology: H:N-M

1) gate
1a) gate (of entrance)
1b) gate (of space inside gate, i.e. marketplace, public meeting place)
1b1) city, town
1c) gate (of palace, royal castle, temple, court of tabernacle)
1d) heaven
