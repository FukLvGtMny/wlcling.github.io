ʾārav אָרַב ‘to ambush’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Morphology: V

ʾārav
1) to lie in wait, ambush, lurk
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to lie in wait
1a2) ambush (participle as subst)
1b) (Piel) ambushers, liers-in-wait (pl. participle)
1c) (Hiphil) to lay an ambush
