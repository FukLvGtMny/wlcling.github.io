šēm שֵׁם ‘name’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive word (perhaps rather from H7760 through the idea of definite and conspicuous position | Roots: שׂום שׂים | In another language: שׂום שׂים | Morphology: H:N-M

1) name
1a) name
1b) reputation, fame, glory
1c) the Name (as designation of God)
1d) memorial, monument
