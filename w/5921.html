ʿal עַל ‘(a) upon (b) upon’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: (properly) the same as H5920 used as a preposition (in the singular or plural often with prefix, or as conjunction with a particle following) | Roots: על | In another language: עלה | Variants: [כִּי] עַל [כֵּן], עַל | Morphology: (a) H:PREP (b) H:PREP

(a) prep
1) upon, on the ground of, according to, on account of, on behalf of, concerning, beside, in addition to, together with, beyond, above, over, by, on to, towards, to, against
1a) upon, on the ground of, on the basis of, on account of, because of, therefore, on behalf of, for the sake of, for, with, in spite of, notwithstanding, concerning, in the matter of, as regards
1b) above, beyond, over (of excess)
1c) above, over (of elevation or pre-eminence)
1d) upon, to, over to, unto, in addition to, together with, with (of addition)
1e) over (of suspension or extension)
1f) by, adjoining, next, at, over, around (of contiguity or proximity)
1g) down upon, upon, on, from, up upon, up to, towards, over towards, to, against (with verbs of motion)
1h) to (as a dative) (b) conj
1) forasmuch as, because that, because, notwithstanding, although
