ʾîš אִישׁ ‘man’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: contracted for H582 (or perhaps rather from an unused root meaning to be extant) | Roots: אנושׁ | In another language: אנשׁ | Morphology: H:N-M

1) man
1a) man, male (in contrast to woman, female)
1b) husband
1c) human being, person (in contrast to God)
1d) servant
1e) mankind
1f) champion
1g) great man
2) whosoever
3) each (adjective)
