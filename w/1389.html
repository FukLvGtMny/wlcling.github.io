gibʿâ גִּבְעָה ‘hill’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: feminine from the same as H1387

Roots: גבע

In another language: גבע

Variants: H:N-F

Meaning
1) hill
1a) hill (lower than a mountain)
1b) as a place of illicit worship
1c) poetic for mountain
1d) used in place names
