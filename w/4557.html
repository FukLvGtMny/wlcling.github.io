mispār מִסְפָּר ‘number’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H5608 | Roots: ספר | In another language: ספר | Morphology: H:N-M

1) number, tale
1a) number
1a1) number
1a2) innumerable (with negative)
1a3) few, numerable (alone)
1a4) by count, in number, according to number (with prep)
1b) recounting, relation
