ʾaššûr אַשּׁוּר ‘Asshur’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: apparently from H833 (in the sense of successful) | Roots: אשׁר אשׁר | In another language: אשׁר | Morphology: N:N-M-P + N:N--L + N:N--LG

Asshur or Assyria = "a step"
n pr m
1) the second son of Shem, eponymous ancestor of the Assyrians
2) the people of Assyria
n pr loc
3) the nation, Assyria
4) the land, Assyria or Asshur
