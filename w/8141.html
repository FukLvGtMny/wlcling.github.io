šānā שָׁנָה ‘year’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H8138 | Roots: שׁנה | In another language: שׁנה | Morphology: H:N-F

1) year
1a) as division of time
1b) as measure of time
1c) as indication of age
1d) a lifetime (of years of life)
