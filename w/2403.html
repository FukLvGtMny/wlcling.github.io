ḥaṭṭāʾâ חַטָּאָה ‘(a) sin (b) sin’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2398

Roots: חטא

In another language: חטא

Variants: חַטָּאת, חַטָּאָה

Morphology: (a) H:N-F (b) H:N-F

(a) 1) sin, sinful thing (b) 1) sin, sin offering
1a) sin
1b) condition of sin, guilt of sin
1c) punishment for sin
1d) sin-offering
1e) purification from sins of ceremonial uncleanness
