yeter יֶ֫תֶר ‘(a) remainder (b) cord’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H3498

Roots: יתר

In another language: יתר

Meaning
(a) 1) remainder, excess, rest, remnant, excellence
1a) remainder, remnant
1b) remainder, rest, other part
1c) excess
1d) abundantly (adv)
1e) abundance, affluence
1f) superiority, excellency (b) 1) cord
