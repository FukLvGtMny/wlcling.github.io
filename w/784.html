ʾēš אֵשׁ ‘fire’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive word

In another language: אשׁ

Morphology: H:N-F

1) fire
1a) fire, flames
1b) supernatural fire (accompanying theophany)
1c) fire (for cooking, roasting, parching)
1d) altar-fire
1e) God's anger (fig.)
