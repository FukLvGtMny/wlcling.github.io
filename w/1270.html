barzel בַּרְזֶל ‘iron’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: perhaps from the root of H1269 | Roots: ברזות | Morphology: H:N-M

1) iron
1a) iron
1a1) iron ore
1a2) as material of furniture, utensils, implements
2) tool of iron
3) harshness, strength, oppression (fig.)
