qedem קֶ֫דֶם ‘1front 2east’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H6923 | Roots: קדם | In another language: קדם | Variants: קֶ֫דֶם, קֵ֫דֶם | Morphology: 1H:N-M 2H:ADV

A 1) east, antiquity, front, that which is before, aforetime
1a) front, from the front or east, in front, mount of the East
1b) ancient time, aforetime, ancient, from of old, earliest time
1c) anciently, of old (adverb)
1d) beginning
1e) east B adv
1) eastward, to or toward the East
