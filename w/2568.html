ḥāmēš חָמֵשׁ ‘five’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive numeral | In another language: חמשׁ | Morphology: H:N

1) five
1a) five (cardinal number)
1b) a multiple of five (with another number)
1c) fifth (ordinal number)
