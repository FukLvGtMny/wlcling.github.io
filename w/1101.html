bālal בָּלַל ‘(a) to mix (b) to feed’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: בלל | Variants: בָּלַל, בָּלַל | Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:V

(a) 1) to mix, mingle, confuse, confound
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to mingle, confuse
1a2) to mix
1b) (Hithpoel) to mix oneself (among others)
1c) (Hiphil) to fade away (b) 1) to give provender, feed (animals)
