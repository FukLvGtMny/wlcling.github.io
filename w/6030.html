ʿûn עוּן ‘(a) to dwell (b) to answer (c) to sing’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: ענה | Variants: עוּן, עָנָה, עָנָה | Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:V (c) H:V

(a) 1) (Qal) to dwell (b) 1) to answer, respond, testify, speak, shout
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to answer, respond to
1a2) to testify, respond as a witness
1b) (Niphal)
1b1) to make answer
1b2) to be answered, receive answer (c) 1) (Qal) to sing, utter tunefully
