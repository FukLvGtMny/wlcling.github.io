ʾāz אָז ‘(a) then (b) after’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a demonstrative adverb | Variants: מֵאָז, אָז | Morphology: (a) H:ADV (b) H:ADV

(a) 1) then, at that time
1a) temporal expressions
1a1) then (past)
1a2) then, if...then (future)
1a3) earlier
1b) logical expressions
1b1) in that case
1b2) that (being so) (b) 1)in time past, of old
2)from time of, since
