אֶפְרַ֫יִם, Hebrew ‘Ephraim’

(language: ), frequency: 180.0, first occurence (encoded): 1041052.0

In another language: פרה ()

Roots: 672.0 (אפרת אפרתה) () ()

Morphology: name

Etymology: [dual of masculine form of H672]

Roots: ◊672¤

Variants: אֶפְרַ֫יִם

Morphology: N:N-M-P + N:N-M-PG

Meaning: Ephraim = "double ash-heap: I shall be doubly fruitful"
1) second son of Joseph, blessed by him and given preference over first son, Manasseh
2) the tribe, Ephraim
3) the mountain country of Ephraim
4) sometimes used name for the northern kingdom (Hosea or Isaiah)
5) a city near Baal-hazor
6) a chief gate of Jerusalem
