lūn לוּן ‘1to lodge 2to grumble’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Variants: לוּן, לוּן | Morphology: V

lūn
A 1) to lodge, stop over, pass the night, abide
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to lodge, pass the night
1a2) to abide, remain (fig.)
1b) (Hiphil) to cause to rest, lodge
1c) (Hithpalpel) to dwell, abide
B 1) to grumble, complain, murmur
1a) (Niphal) to grumble, murmur
1b) (Hiphil) to complain, cause to grumble
