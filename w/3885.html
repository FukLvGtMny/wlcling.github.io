lûn לוּן ‘(a) to lodge (b) to grumble’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root

In another language: לון לין

Variants: לוּן, לוּן

Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:V

(a) 1) to lodge, stop over, pass the night, abide
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to lodge, pass the night
1a2) to abide, remain (fig.)
1b) (Hiphil) to cause to rest, lodge
1c) (Hithpalpel) to dwell, abide (b) 1) to grumble, complain, murmur
1a) (Niphal) to grumble, murmur
1b) (Hiphil) to complain, cause to grumble
