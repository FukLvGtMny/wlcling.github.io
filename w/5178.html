nǝḥōšet נְחֹ֫שֶׁת ‘(a) bronze (b) lust’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: for H5154 | Roots: נחושׁה נחושׁה | In another language: נחשׁ | Variants: נְחֹ֫שֶׁת, נְחֹ֫שֶׁת | Morphology: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

(a) 1) copper, bronze
1a) copper (ore), bronze (as copper alloy)
1b) fetters (of copper or bronze)
1c) copper (as value) (b) 1) lust, harlotry
