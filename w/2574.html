ḥămāt חֲמָת ‘Hamath’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the same as H2346 | Roots: חומה | Morphology: N:N--L + N:N-M-P

Hamath = "fortress"
n pr loc
1) the principle city of upper Syria in the valley of the Orontes
n pr m
2) father of the house of Rechab
