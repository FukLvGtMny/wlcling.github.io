ḥokmâ חׇכְמָה ‘wisdom’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2449 | Roots: חכם | In another language: חכם | Morphology: H:N-F

1) wisdom
1a) skill (in war)
1b) wisdom (in administration)
1c) shrewdness, wisdom
1d) wisdom, prudence (in religious affairs)
1e) wisdom (ethical and religious)
