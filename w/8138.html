šānā שָׁנָה ‘1to change 2to repeat’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: שׁנה | Variants: שָׁנָה, שָׁנָה | Morphology: H:V

A 1) change, alter
1a) (Qal) to change
1b) (Piel) to change, alter
1c) (Pual) to be changed, mollified
1d) (Hithpael) to disguise oneself
B 1) to repeat, do again
1a) (Qal) to do again
1b) (Niphal) to be repeated
