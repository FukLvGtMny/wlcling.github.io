yām יָם ‘sea’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to roar | In another language: ים | Morphology: H:N-M

1) sea
1a) Mediterranean Sea
1b) Red Sea
1c) Dead Sea
1d) Sea of Galilee
1e) sea (general)
1f) mighty river (Nile)
1g) the sea (the great basin in the temple court)
1h) seaward, west, westward
