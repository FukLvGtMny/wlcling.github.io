may מַי ‘water’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: dual of a primitive noun (but used in a singular sense) | Morphology: H:N-M

1) water, waters
1a) water
1b) water of the feet, urine
1c) of danger, violence, transitory things, refreshment (fig.)
