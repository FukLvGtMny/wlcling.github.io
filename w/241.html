ʾōzen אֹ֫זֶן ‘ear’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H238 | Roots: אזן | Morphology: N-F

ʾōzen
1) ear, as part of the body
2) ear, as organ of hearing
3) (subjective) to uncover the ear to reveal; the receiver of divine revelation
