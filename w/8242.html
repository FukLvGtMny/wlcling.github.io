śaq שַׂק ‘sackcloth’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H8264 | Roots: שׁקק | Morphology: N-M

1) mesh, sackcloth, sack, sacking
1a) sack (for grain)
1b) sackcloth
1b1) worn in mourning or humiliation
1b2) same material spread out to lie on
