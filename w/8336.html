šēš שֵׁשׁ ‘(a) alabaster (b) linen’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: for H7893

Roots: שׁישׁ

Variants: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

Meaning
(a) 1) alabaster, similar stone, marble, material of pavement (b) 1) something bleached white, byssus, linen, fine linen
