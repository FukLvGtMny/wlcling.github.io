maḥăneh מַחֲנֶה ‘camp’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2583 | Roots: חנה | In another language: חנה | Morphology: H:N-M

1) encampment, camp
1a) camp, place of encampment
1b) camp of armed host, army camp
1c) those who encamp, company, body of people
