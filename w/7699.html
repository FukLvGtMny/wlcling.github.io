šad שַׁד ‘(a) breast (b) breast’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably from H7736 (in its original sense) contracted

Roots: שׁוד

In another language: שׁוד

Variants: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

Meaning
(a) 1) breast, bosom, (female) breast
1a) breast (of woman)
1b) breast (of animal)
1c) breast (of both human and animal) (b) 1) breast, bosom, (female) breast
1a) breast (of woman)
1b) breast (of animal)
1c) breast (of both human and animal)
