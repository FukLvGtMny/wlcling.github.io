šǝmōneh שְׁמֹנֶה ‘eight’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: apparently from H8082 through the idea of plumpness | Roots: שׁמן | Morphology: H:N

1) eight, eighth
1a) eight (as cardinal number)
1b) eighth (as ordinal number)
1c) in combination with other numbers
