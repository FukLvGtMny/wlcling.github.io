ʿēśāw עֵשָׂו ‘Esau’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: apparently a form of the passive participle of H6213 in the original sense of handling | Roots: עשׂה | In another language: עשׂה | Morphology: N:N-M-P

Esau = "hairy"
1) eldest son of Isaac and Rebecca and twin brother of Jacob; sold the birthright for food when he was hungry and the divine blessing went to Jacob; progenitor of the Arab peoples
