pǝrî פְּרִי ‘fruit’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H6509

Roots: פרה

In another language: פרה

Morphology: H:N-M

Meaning
1) fruit
1a) fruit, produce (of the ground)
1b) fruit, offspring, children, progeny (of the womb)
1c) fruit (of actions) (fig.)
