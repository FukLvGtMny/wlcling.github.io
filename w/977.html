bāḥar בָּחַר ‘to choose’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: בחר | Morphology: H:V

1) to choose, elect, decide for
1a) (Qal) to choose
1b) (Niphal) to be chosen
1c) (Pual) to be chosen, selected
