zebaḥ זֶ֫בַח ‘sacrifice’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2076

Roots: זבח

In another language: זבח

Morphology: H:N-M

1) sacrifice
1a) sacrifices of righteousness
1b) sacrifices of strife
1c) sacrifices to dead things
1d) the covenant sacrifice
1e) the passover
1f) annual sacrifice
1g) thank offering
