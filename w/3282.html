yaʿan יַעַן ‘because’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to pay attention

Variants: H:CONJ

Meaning
conj
1) because, therefore, because that, on account of
prep
2) because of, on account of
3) why (with interrogative pron)
