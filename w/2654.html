ḥāfēṣ חָפֵץ ‘1to delight in 2to bend’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: חפץ | Variants: חָפֵץ, חָפַץ | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to delight in, take pleasure in, desire, be pleased with
1a) (Qal)
1a1) of men
1a1a) to take pleasure in, delight in
1a1b) to delight, desire, be pleased to do
1a2) of God
1a2a) to delight in, have pleasure in
1a2b) to be pleased to do
B 1) to move, bend down
1a) (Qal) to bend down
