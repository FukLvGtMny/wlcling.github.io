kōh כֹּה ‘thus’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the prefix k and H1931 | Roots: הוא היא | In another language: הוא היא | Morphology: H:ADV

1) thus, here, in this manner
1a) thus, so
1b) here, here and there
1c) until now, until now...until then, meanwhile
