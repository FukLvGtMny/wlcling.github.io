ʾĕdôm אֱדוֹם ‘Edom’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H122 | Roots: אדום | In another language: אדם (940) | Morphology: N:N-M-P + N:N--L

Edom = "red"
1) Edom
2) Edomite, Idumean-descendants of Esau
3) land of Edom, Idumea-land south and south east of Palestine
