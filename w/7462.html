bēt-ʿēqed hārōʿīm בֵּית־עֵ֫קֶד הָרֹעִים ‘1of Shepherds 2to pasture 3to accompany 4to befriend’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Variants: רָעָה, בֵּית־עֵ֫קֶד הָרֹעִים, רָעָה | Morphology: 1N:N--L 2H:V 3H:V 4H:V

bēt-ʿēqed hārōʿīm
A Ra'ah = "pasturing"
n pr loc
1) perh. "binding-house of the shepherds"
B 1) to pasture, tend, graze, feed
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to tend, pasture
1a1a) to shepherd
1a1b) of ruler, teacher (fig)
1a1c) of people as flock (fig)
1a1d) shepherd, herdsman (subst)
1a2) to feed, graze
1a2a) of cows, sheep etc (literal)
1a2b) of idolater, Israel as flock (fig)
1b) (Hiphil) shepherd, shepherdess
C 1) to associate with, be a friend of (meaning probable)
1a) (Qal) to associate with
1b) (Hithpael) to be companions
D 1) (Piel) to be a special friend
