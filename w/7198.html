qešet קֶ֫שֶׁת ‘bow’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7185 in the original sense (of H6983) of bending

Roots: קשׁה קושׁ

In another language: קשׁה

Variants: H:N-F

Meaning
1) bow
1a) bow (for hunting, battle)
1b) bowmen, archers
1c) bow (fig. of might)
1d) rainbow
