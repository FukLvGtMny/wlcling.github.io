šāwǝʾ שָׁוְא ‘vanity’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the same as H7722 in the sense of desolating | Roots: שׁוא שׁואה שׁואה | Morphology: H:N-M

1) emptiness, vanity, falsehood
1a) emptiness, nothingness, vanity
1b) emptiness of speech, lying
1c) worthlessness (of conduct)
