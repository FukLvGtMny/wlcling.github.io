אֵל, ‘god’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: [shortened from H352]

Roots: איל

frequency: 9, first occurence (encoded): 1019008

In another language: אול ()

Morphology: noun masc

Roots: ◊352¤

Variants: אֵל

Morphology: H:N-M

Meaning: 1) god, god-like one, mighty one
1a) mighty men, men of rank, mighty heroes
1b) angels
1c) god, false god, (demons, imaginations)
1d) God, the one true God, Jehovah
2) mighty things in nature
3) strength, power
