beṭen בֶּ֫טֶן ‘belly’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root probably meaning to be hollow | Morphology: H:N-F

1) belly, womb, body
1a) belly, abdomen
1a1) as seat of hunger
1a2) as seat of mental faculties
1a3) of depth of Sheol (fig.)
1b) womb
