ṭāhôr טָהוֹר ‘pure’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2891

Roots: טהר

In another language: טהר

Variants: H:A

Meaning
1) pure, clean
1a) clean (ceremonially-of animals)
1b) pure (physically)
1c) pure, clean (morally, ethically)
