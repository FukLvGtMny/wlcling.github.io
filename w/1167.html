baʿal בַּעַל ‘master’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H1166 | Roots: בעל | In another language: בעל | Morphology: H:N-M

1a) owner
1b) a husband
1c) citizens, inhabitants
1d) rulers, lords
1e) (noun of relationship used to characterise-ie, master of dreams)
