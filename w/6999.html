qāṭar קָטַר ‘(a) to offer (b) incense (c) incense-altar’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root (identical with H7000 through the idea of fumigation in a close place and perhaps thus driving out the occupants) | Roots: קטר | In another language: קטר | Variants: קָטַר, מְקַטֵרָה, מֻקְטָר | Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:N-M (c) H:N-F

(a) v
1) to sacrifice, burn incense, burn sacrifices, make sacrifices smoke
1a) (Piel)
1a1) to make sacrifices smoke
1a2) to sacrifice
1b) (Pual) to smoke a sacrifice
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to make sacrifices smoke
1c2) to cause incense to smoke, offer incense
1c3) to make smoke upon
1d) (Hophal) to be made to smoke (b) v
1) incense (c) 1) incense-altar
