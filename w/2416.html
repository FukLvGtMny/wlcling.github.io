ḥay חַי ‘(a) alive (b) kinsfolk (c) living thing (d) community (e) life’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2421 | Roots: חיה | In another language: חיה | Variants: חַיָּה, חַי, חַיִּים | Morphology: (a) H:A (b) H:N-M (c) H:N-F (d) H:N-F (e) H:N-M

(a) adj
1) living, alive
1a) green (of vegetation)
1b) flowing, fresh (of water)
1c) lively, active (of man)
1d) reviving (of the springtime) (b) 1) kinsfolk (c) 1) living thing, animal
1a) animal
1b) life
1c) appetite
1d) revival, renewal (d) 1) community (e) 1) life (abstract emphatic)
1a) life
1b) sustenance, maintenance
