ḥāzāq חָזָק ‘strong’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2388 | Roots: חזק | Morphology: A

ḥāzāq
1) strong, stout, mighty
1a) strong
1a1) severe, sharp, hot
1a2) firm, hard
1b) a strong one (subst)
