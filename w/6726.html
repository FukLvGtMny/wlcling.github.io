ṣiyyôn צִיּוֹן ‘Zion’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: the same (regularly) as H6725

Roots: ציון

In another language: ציה

Variants: N:N--L

Meaning
Zion = "parched place"
1) another name for Jerusalem especially in the prophetic books
