ʾoxlā אׇכְלָה ‘food’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: feminine of H401 | Roots: אוכל אוכל | Morphology: H:N-F

1) food
1a) food, eating
1b) object of devouring, consuming
1b1) by wild beasts (figurative)
1b2) in fire
1b3) of judgment (figurative)
