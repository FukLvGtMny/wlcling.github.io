yādā יָדָה ‘to give thanks’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root; used only as denominative from H3027 | Roots: יד | In another language: ידה | Morphology: H:V

1) to throw, shoot, cast
1a) (Qal) to shoot (arrows)
1b) (Piel) to cast, cast down, throw down
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to give thanks, laud, praise
1c2) to confess, confess (the name of God)
1d) (Hithpael)
1d1) to confess (sin)
1d2) to give thanks
