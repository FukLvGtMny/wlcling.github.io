ʾāṣēl אָצֵל ‘(a) Azel (b) Azal’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H680 | Roots: אצל | Variants: אָצֵל, אָצֵל | Morphology: (a) N:N-M-P (b) N:N--L

(a) Azel = "reserved"
n pr m (CLBL)
1) a Benjamite descendant of Saul and Jonathan (b) Azal = "proximity: he has reserved"
n pr loc
1) a place near Jerusalem, site presently unknown
