בַּ֫עַד, ‘about/through/for’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: [from H5704 with prepositional prefix]

Roots: עד

frequency: 105, first occurence (encoded): 1007016

In another language: ()

Morphology: Preposition

Roots: ◊5704¤

Variants: בַּ֫עַד

Morphology: H:PREP

Meaning: 1) behind, through, round about, on behalf of, away from, about
1a) through (of action)
1b) behind (with verbs of shutting)
1c) about (with verbs of fencing)
1d) on behalf of (metaph. especially with Hithpael)
