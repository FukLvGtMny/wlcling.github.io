baʿad בַּ֫עַד ‘about/through/for’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H5704 with prepositional prefix | Roots: עד | Morphology: PREP

baʿad1) behind, through, round about, on behalf of, away from, about
1a) through (of action)
1b) behind (with verbs of shutting)
1c) about (with verbs of fencing)
1d) on behalf of (metaph. especially with Hithpael)
