šāḥâ שָׁחָה ‘to bow’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: שׁחה | Morphology: H:V

1) to bow down
1a) (Qal) to bow down
1b)(Hiphil) to depress (fig)
1c) (Hithpael)
1c1) to bow down, prostrate oneself
1c1a) before superior in homage
1c1b) before God in worship
1c1c) before false gods
1c1d) before angel
