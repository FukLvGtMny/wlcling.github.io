zāmar זָמַר ‘to sing’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root (perhaps ident. with H2168 through the idea of striking with the fingers) | Roots: זמר | Morphology: V

zāmar
1) to sing, sing praise, make music
1a) (Piel)
1a1) to make music, sing
1a2) to play a musical instrument
