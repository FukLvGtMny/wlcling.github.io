pādâ פָּדָה ‘to ransom’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: פדה | Morphology: H:V

1) to ransom, redeem, rescue, deliver
1a) (Qal) to ransom
1b) (Niphal) to be ransomed
1c) (Hiphil) to allow one to be ransomed
1d) (Hophal) redeemed
