māṭar מָטַר ‘to rain’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: מטר | Morphology: H:V

1) to rain
1a) (Niphal) to be rained on or upon
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to rain, send rain
1b2) to rain hail, send hail
