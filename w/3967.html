mēʾā מֵאָה ‘hundred’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: (properly) a primitive numeral | Morphology: H:N-F

1) hundred
1a) as simple number
1b) as part of larger number
1c) as a fraction-one one-hundredth (1/100)
