(a) to shine (b) to boast (c) to be foolish

Word: הָלַל (language: H), frequency: 165.0, first occurence (encoded): 1012015.0

In another language: הלל ()

Roots: () () ()

Morphology: verb

Etymology: [a primitive root]

Roots:

Variants: (a) הָלַל (b) הָלַל (c) הָלַל

Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:V (c) H:V

Meaning: (a) 1) to shine
1a) (Qal) to shine (fig. of God's favour)
1b) (Hiphil) to flash forth light (b) 1) to praise, boast, be boastful
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to be boastful
1a2) boastful ones, boasters (participle)
1b) (Piel)
1b1) to praise
1b2) to boast, make a boast
1c) (Pual)
1c1) to be praised, be made praiseworthy, be commended, be worthy of praise
1d) (Hithpael) to boast, glory, make one's boast (c) 1) (Poel) to make a fool of, make into a fool
1f) (Hithpoel) to act madly, act like a madman
