hārag הָרַג ‘to kill’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Morphology: V

hārag1) to kill, slay, murder, destroy, murderer, slayer, out of hand
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to kill, slay
1a2) to destroy, ruin
1b) (Niphal) to be killed
1c) (Pual) to be killed, be slain
