māšal מָשַׁל ‘to rule’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Morphology: V

māšal1) to rule, have dominion, reign
1a) (Qal) to rule, have dominion
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to cause to rule
1b2) to exercise dominion
