zāhāv זָהָב ‘gold’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to shimmer | In another language: זהב | Morphology: H:N-M

1) gold
1a) as precious metal
1b) as a measure of weight
1c) of brilliance, splendour (fig.)
