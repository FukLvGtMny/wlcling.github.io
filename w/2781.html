ḥerpâ חֶרְפָּה ‘reproach’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2778 | Roots: חרף | In another language: חרף | Morphology: H:N-F

1) reproach, scorn
1a) taunt, scorn (upon enemy)
1b) reproach (resting upon condition of shame, disgrace)
1c) a reproach (an object)
