ḥebrôn חֶבְרוֹן ‘Hebron’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H2267 | Roots: חבר | Variants: חֶבְרוֹן, חֶבְרוֹן | Morphology: 1N:N--L 2N:N-M-P

A Hebron = "association"
n pr loc
1) a city in south Judah approx 20 south of Jerusalem and approx 20 miles (30 km) north of Beersheba and near where Abraham built an altar B Hebron = "association"
n pr m
1) the 3rd son of Kohath and grandson of Levi
2) a descendant of Caleb
