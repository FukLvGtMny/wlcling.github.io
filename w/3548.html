kōhēn כֹּהֵן ‘priest’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: active participle of H3547 | Roots: כהן | Morphology: N-M

kōhēn
1) priest, principal officer or chief ruler
1a) priest-king (Melchizedek, Messiah)
1b) pagan priests
1c) priests of Jehovah
1d) Levitical priests
1e) Zadokite priests
1f) Aaronic priests
1g) the high priest
