ʾărām אֲרָם ‘Aram’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from the same as H759 | Roots: ארמון | Morphology: N:N-M-P + N:N-M-PG

Aram or Arameans = "exalted"
n pr m
1) Aram or Syriathe nation
2) the Syrian or Aramean people
Aram = "exalted"
n m
3) fifth son of Shem
4) a grandson of Nahor
5) a descendant of Asher
