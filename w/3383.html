yardēn יַרְדֵּן ‘Jordan’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H3381

Roots: ירד

In another language: ירד

Morphology: N:N--L

Meaning
Jordan = "descender"
1) the river of Palestine running from the roots of Anti-Lebanon to the Dead Sea a distance of approx 200 miles (320 km)
