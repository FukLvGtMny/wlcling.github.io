yēš יֵשׁ ‘be’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: perhaps from an unused root meaning to stand out, or exist

Morphology: H:PRT

Meaning
1) being, existence, substance, there is or are
1a) substance
1b) existence
1c) there is or are
