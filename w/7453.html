rēaʿ רֵעַ ‘neighbor’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7462 | Roots: רעה | Morphology: N-M

1) friend, companion, fellow, another person
1a) friend, intimate
1b) fellow, fellow-citizen, another person (weaker sense)
1c) other, another (reciprocal phrase)
