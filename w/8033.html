šām שָׁם ‘there’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive particle (rather from the relative pronoun, H834) | Roots: אשׁר | Morphology: ADV

1) there, thither
1a) there
1b) thither (after verbs of motion)
1c) from there, thence
1d) then (as an adverb of time)
