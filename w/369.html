ʾayin אַ֫יִן ‘nothing’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: as if from a primitive root meaning to be nothing or not exist | In another language: אין | Morphology: H:NP

1) nothing, not, nought n
1a) nothing, nought neg
1b) not
1c) to have not (of possession) adv
1d) without w/prep
1e) for lack of
