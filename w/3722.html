kipper כִּפֶּר ‘1to atone 2to cover’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: כפר | Variants: כִּפֶּר, כָּפַר | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to cover, purge, make an atonement, make reconciliation
1a) (Piel)
1a1) to cover over, pacify, propitiate
1a2) to cover over, atone for sin, make atonement for
1a3) to cover over, atone for sin and persons by legal rites
1b) (Pual)
1b1) to be covered over
1b2) to be atoned for
1c) (Hithpael) to be covered
B 1)(Qal) to coat or cover with pitch
