bāšān בָּשָׁן ‘Bashan’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: of uncertain derivation

Variants: N:N--L

Meaning
Bashan = "fruitful"
1) a district east of the Jordan known for its fertility which was given to the half-tribe of Manasseh
