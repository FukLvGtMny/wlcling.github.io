ʾădōnāy אֲדֹנָי ‘Lord’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: am emphatic form of H113 | Roots: אדון אדון | In another language: אדן (868) | Morphology: H:N-M

1) my lord, lord
1a) of men
1b) of God
2) Lord-title, spoken in place of Yahweh in Jewish display of reverence
