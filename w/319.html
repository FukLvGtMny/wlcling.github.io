ʾaḥărît אַחֲרִית ‘end’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H310 | Roots: אחר | In another language: אחר | Morphology: H:N-F

1) after part, end
1a) end, issue, event
1b) latter time (prophetic for future time)
1c) posterity
1d) last, hindermost
