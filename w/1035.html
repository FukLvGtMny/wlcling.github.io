bêt leḥem בֵּית לֶ֫חֶם ‘Beth-lehem’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H1004 and H3899 | Roots: בית לחם | Morphology: N:N--L

Beth-lehem = "house of bread (food)"
1) a city in Judah, birthplace of David
2) a place in Zebulun
