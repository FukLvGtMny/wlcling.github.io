bāṭaḥ בָּטַח ‘to trust’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: בטח | Morphology: H:V

1) to trust
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to trust, trust in
1a2) to have confidence, be confident
1a3) to be bold
1a4) to be secure
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to cause to trust, make secure
2) (TWOT) to feel safe, be careless
