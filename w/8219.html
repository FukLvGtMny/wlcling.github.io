šǝpēlâ שְׁפֵלָה ‘lowland’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H8213

Roots: שׁפל

In another language: שׁפל

Morphology: N:N--L

Meaning
1) lowland, valley
1a) lowland
1a1) strip west of Judean mountains (technical term)
1a1a) the Shephelah
1a2) strip near coast north of Carmel
