tāmīd תָּמִיד ‘continually’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to stretch | Morphology: N-M

tāmīd1) continuity, perpetuity, to stretch
1a) continually, continuously (as adverb)
1b) continuity (subst)
