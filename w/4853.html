maśśāʾ מַשָּׂא ‘(a) burden (b) oracle’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H5375 | Roots: נשׂא נסה | In another language: נשׂא | Variants: מַשָּׂא, מַשָּׂא | Morphology: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

(a) 1) load, bearing, tribute, burden, lifting
1a) load, burden
1b) lifting, uplifting, that to which the soul lifts itself up
1c) bearing, carrying
1d) tribute, that which is carried or brought or borne (b) 1) utterance, oracle, burden
