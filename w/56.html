ʾābal אָבַל ‘to mourn’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: אבל (96) | Morphology: H:V

1) to mourn, lament
1a) (Qal) to mourn, lament
1a1) of humans
1a2) of inanimate objects (fig.)
1a2a) of gates
1a2b) of land
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to mourn, cause to mourn (fig.)
1c) (Hithpael)
1c1) to mourn
1c2) play the mourner
