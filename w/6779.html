ṣāmaḥ צָמַח ‘to spring’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: צמח | Morphology: H:V

1) to sprout, spring up, grow up
1a)(Qal)
1a1) to sprout, spring up
1a1a) of plants
1a1b) of hair
1a1c) of speech (fig.)
1b) (Piel) to grow abundantly or thickly
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to cause to grow
1c2) to cause to sprout
