bǝrîḥa בְּרִיחַ ‘bar’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H1272 | Roots: ברח | In another language: ברח | Morphology: H:N-M

1) bar
1a) of wood
1b) of city gates
2) of tribulation, a fortress, of the earth as a prison (fig.)
