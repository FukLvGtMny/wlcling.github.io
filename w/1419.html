gādôl גָּדוֹל ‘(a) great (b) Haggedolim’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H1431 | Roots: גדל | In another language: גדל | Variants: הַגְּדוֹלִים, גָּדוֹל | Morphology: (a) H:A (b) N:N-M-P

(a) adj
1) great
1a) large (in magnitude and extent)
1b) in number
1c) in intensity
1d) loud (in sound)
1e) older (in age)
1f) in importance
1f1) important things
1f2) great, distinguished (of men)
1f3) God Himself (of God)
subst
1g) great things
1h) haughty things
1i) greatness (b) Haggedolim = "the great one"
n pr m
1) (CLBL) Haggedolim, the great man?, father of Zabdiel
