ʾādām אָדָם ‘man’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H119 | Roots: אדם (940) | Morphology: N-M

ʾādām
1) man, mankind
1a) man, human being
1b) man, mankind (much more frequently intended sense in OT)
1c) Adam, first man
1d) city in Jordan valley
