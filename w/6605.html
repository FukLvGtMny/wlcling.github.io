pātaḥ פָּתַח ‘1to open 2to engrave’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: פתח | Variants: פָּתַח, פָּתַח | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to open
1a) (Qal) to open
1b) (Niphal) to be opened, be let loose, be thrown open
1c) (Piel)
1c1) to free
1c2) to loosen
1c3) to open, open oneself
1d) (Hithpael) to loose oneself
B 1) to carve, engrave
1a) (Piel) to engrave
1b) (Pual) to be engraved
