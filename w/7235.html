rābâ רָבָה ‘(a) to multiply (b) to shoot’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: רבה | Variants: רָבָה, רָבָה | Morphology: (a) H:V (b) H:V

(a) 1) be or become great, be or become many, be or become much, be or become numerous
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to become many, become numerous, multiply (of people, animals, things)
1a2) to be or grow great
1b) (Piel) to make large, enlarge, increase, become many
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to make much, make many, have many
1c1a) to multiply, increase
1c1b) to make much to do, do much in respect of, transgress greatly
1c1c) to increase greatly or exceedingly
1c2) to make great, enlarge, do much (b) 1) (Qal) to shoot
