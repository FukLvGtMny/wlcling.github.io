mālak מָלַךְ ‘(a) to reign (b) to advise’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root

In another language: מלך

Variants: (a) H:V (b) H:V

Meaning
(a) 1) to be or become king or queen, reign
1a) (Qal) to be or become king or queen, reign
1b) (Hiphil) to make one king or queen, cause to reign
1c) (Hophal) to be made king or queen (b) 1) to counsel, advise
1a) (Niphal) to consider
