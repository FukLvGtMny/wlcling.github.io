mālak מָלַךְ ‘1to reign 2to advise’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: מלך | Variants: מָלַךְ, מָלַךְ | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to be or become king or queen, reign
1a) (Qal) to be or become king or queen, reign
1b) (Hiphil) to make one king or queen, cause to reign
1c) (Hophal) to be made king or queen
B 1) to counsel, advise
1a) (Niphal) to consider
