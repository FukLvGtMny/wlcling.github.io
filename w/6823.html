ṣāfā צָפָה ‘to overlay’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root (probably identical with H6822 through the idea of expansion in outlook, transferring to action) | Roots: צפה | In another language: צפה | Morphology: H:V

1) to lay out, lay over, overlay, cover
1a) (Piel) to overlay, plate, stud
1b) (Pual) to be laid over
