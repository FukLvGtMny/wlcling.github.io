מִצְרַ֫יִם [N:N--L]
Egypt

Egypt = "land of the Copts"
n pr loc
1) a country at the northeastern section of Africa, adjacent to Palestine, and through which the Nile flows
Egyptians = "double straits"
adj
2) the inhabitants or natives of Egypt
