sûs סוּס ‘(a) swallow (b) horse’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to skip (properly, for joy) | In another language: סוס | Variants: סוּס, סוּס | Morphology: (a) H:N-M (b) H:N-M

(a) 1) swallow, swift (b) 1) horse
1a) chariot horses
