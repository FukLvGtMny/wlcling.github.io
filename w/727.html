ʾărôn אֲרוֹן ‘ark’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H717 (in the sense of gathering)

Roots: ארה

In another language: ארה

Variants: H:N-M

Meaning
1) chest, ark
1a) money chest
1b) Ark of the Covenant
2)(TWOT) coffin
