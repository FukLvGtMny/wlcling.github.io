ʿōref עֹ֫רֶף ‘neck’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H6202 | Roots: ערף | Morphology: H:N-M

1) neck, back of the neck, back
1a) back of the neck
1a1) of fleeing foe
1a2) of apostasy (fig.)
1b) stiff of neck, obstinate (fig.)
