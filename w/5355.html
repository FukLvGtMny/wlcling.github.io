nāqî נָקִי ‘(a) innocent (b) innocent’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H5352

Roots: נקה

In another language: נקה

Variants: (a) H:A (b) H:A

Meaning
(a) 1) clean, free from, exempt, clear, innocent
1a) free from guilt, clean, innocent
1b) free from punishment
1c) free or exempt from obligations (b) 1) innocent
