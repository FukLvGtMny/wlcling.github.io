māḥā מָחָה ‘1to wipe 2to strike 3be marrow’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: מחה | Variants: מָחָה, מָחָה | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to wipe, wipe out
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to wipe
1a2) to blot out, obliterate
1a3) to blot out, exterminate
1b) (Niphal)
1b1) to be wiped out
1b2) to be blotted out
1b3) to be exterminated
1c) (Hiphil) to blot out (from memory)
B 1) (Qal) to strike
C 1) (Pual) full of marrow (participle)
