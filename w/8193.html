śāfā שָׂפָה ‘lip’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably from H5595 or H8192 through the idea of termination | Roots: ספה שׁפה | Morphology: N-F

śāfā
1) lip, language, speech, shore, bank, brink, brim, side, edge, border, binding
1a) lip (as body part)
1b) language
1c) edge, shore, bank (of cup, sea, river, etc)
