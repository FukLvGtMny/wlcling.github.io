ʾelep אֶ֫לֶף ‘thousand’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: properly, the same as H504 | Roots: אלף | In another language: אלף | Morphology: H:N-M

1) a thousand
1a) as numeral
2) a thousand, company
2a) as a company of men under one leader, troops
