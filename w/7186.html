qāšeh קָשֶׁה ‘severe’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H7185 | Roots: קשׁה | In another language: קשׁה | Morphology: H:A

1) hard, cruel, severe, obstinate
1a) hard, difficult
1b) severe
1c) fierce, intense, vehement
1d) stubborn, stiff of neck, stiff-necked
1e) rigorous (of battle)
