ʾeḥād אֶחָד ‘one’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a numeral from H258 | Roots: אחד | In another language: אחד | Morphology: H:A

1) one (number)
1a) one (number)
1b) each, every
1c) a certain
1d) an (indefinite article)
1e) only, once, once for all
1f) one...another, the one...the other, one after another, one by one
1g) first
1h) eleven (in combination), eleventh (ordinal)
