derek דֶּ֫רֶךְ ‘way’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H1869 | Roots: דרך | In another language: דרך | Morphology: H:N-M

1a) road, way, path
1b) journey
1c) direction
1d) manner, habit, way
1e) of course of life (fig.)
1f) of moral character (fig.)
