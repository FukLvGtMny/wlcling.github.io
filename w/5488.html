sûp סוּף ‘reed’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably of Egyptian origin. Compare H5489 | Morphology: H:N-M + N:N--L

1) reed, rush, water plant
1a) rushes
1b) sea of rushes
1b1) of Red Sea
1b2) of arms of Red Sea
1b3) of Gulf of Suez
1b4) of sea from straits to Gulf of Akaba
