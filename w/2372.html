ḥāzâ חָזָה ‘to see’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: חזה | Morphology: H:V

1) to see, perceive, look, behold, prophesy, provide
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to see, behold
1a2) to see as a seer in the ecstatic state
1a3) to see, perceive
1a3a) with the intelligence
1a3b) to see (by experience)
1a3c) to provide
