bāxā בָּכָה ‘to weep’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: בכה | Morphology: H:V

1) to weep, bewail, cry, shed tears
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to weep (in grief, humiliation, or joy)
1a2) to weep bitterly (with cognate acc.)
1a3) to weep upon (embrace and weep)
1a4) to bewail
1b) (Piel) participle
1b1) lamenting
1b2) bewailing
