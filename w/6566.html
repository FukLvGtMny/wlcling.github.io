pāraś פָּרַשׂ ‘to spread’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: פּרשׂ | Morphology: H:V

1) to spread, spread out, stretch, break in pieces
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to spread out, display
1a2) to spread over
1b) (Niphal) to be scattered, be spread out
1c) (Piel)
1c1) to spread out
1c1) to scatter
