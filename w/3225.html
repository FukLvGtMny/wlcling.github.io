yāmîn יָמִין ‘right’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H3231 | Roots: ימן | In another language: ימן | Morphology: H:N-F

1) right, right hand, right side
1a) right hand
1b) right (of direction)
1c) south (the direction of the right hand when facing East)
