lāḥam לָחַם ‘1to fight 2to feed on’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: לחם | Variants: לָחַם, לָחַם | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to fight, do battle, make war
1a) (Qal) to fight, do battle
1b) (Niphal) to engage in battle, wage war
B 1) (Qal) to eat, use as food
