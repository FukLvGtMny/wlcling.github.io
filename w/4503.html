minḥâ מִנְחָה ‘offering’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from an unused root meaning to apportion, i.e. bestow

In another language: מנחה

Variants: H:N-F

Meaning
1) gift, tribute, offering, present, oblation, sacrifice, meat offering
1a) gift, present
1b) tribute
1c) offering (to God)
1d) grain offering
