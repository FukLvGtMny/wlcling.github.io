maʿăśeh מַעֲשֶׂה ‘deed’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: from H6213 | Roots: עשׂה | In another language: עשׂה | Morphology: H:N-M

1) deed, work
1a) deed, thing done, act
1b) work, labour
1c) business, pursuit
1d) undertaking, enterprise
1e) achievement
1f) deeds, works (of deliverance and judgment)
1g) work, thing made
1h) work (of God)
1i) product
