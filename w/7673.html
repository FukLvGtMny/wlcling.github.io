šāvat שָׁבַת ‘1to cease 2to keep’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | Variants: שָׁבַת, שָׁבַת | Morphology: V

šāvat
A 1) to cease, desist, rest
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to cease
1a2) to rest, desist (from labour)
1b) (Niphal) to cease
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to cause to cease, put an end to
1c2) to exterminate, destroy
1c3) to cause to desist from
1c4) to remove
1c5) to cause to fail
B 1) (Qal) to keep or observe the sabbath
