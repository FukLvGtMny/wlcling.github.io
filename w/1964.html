hēxāl הֵיכָל ‘temple’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: probably from H3201 (in the sense of capacity) | Roots: יכול יכול | Morphology: N-M

hēxāl1) palace, temple, nave, sanctuary
1a) palace
1b) temple (palace of God as king)
1c) hall, nave (of Ezekiel's temple)
1d) temple (of heavenly temple)
