yārāʾ יָרָא ‘1to shoot 2to shoot/show’ (Hebrew)

Etymology: a primitive root | In another language: ירה ירא | Variants: יָרָה, יָרָא | Morphology: H:V

A 1) to shoot, pour
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to shoot arrows
1b) (Hiphil)
1b1) to shoot at
1b2) part. shooter, archer
B 1) to throw, shoot, cast, pour
1a) (Qal)
1a1) to throw, cast
1a2) to cast, lay, set
1a3) to shoot arrows
1a4) to throw water, rain
1b) (Niphal) to be shot through (with arrows)
1c) (Hiphil)
1c1) to throw, cast
1c2) to shoot
1c3) to point out, show
1c4) to direct, teach, instruct
1c5) to throw water, rain
